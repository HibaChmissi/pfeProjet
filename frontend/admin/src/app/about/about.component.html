About

Sagemcom is a French industrial group, world leader in high added-value communicating terminals and solutions for broadband, audio video solutions, and energy markets.

Sagemcom designs, manufactures, and supplies its products around the world, using its own factories and industrial partners on all continents.

The headcount of 6,500 employees works in more than 50 countries. 30% of Sagemcom's capital is owned by its employees. Since January 2022, Sagemcom has become a "mission-driven company".
