About

Sagemcom is a French industrial group, world leader in high added-value communicating terminals and solutions...

Sagemcom designs, manufactures, and supplies its products around the world, using its own factories and industrial partners on all continents.

The headcount of 6,500 employees works in more than 50 countries. 30% of Sagemcom's capital is owned by its employees. Since January 2022, Sagemcom has become a "mission-driven company".

Services

Energy & Telecom

With its experience in telecommunications and energy management that stretches back more than 25 years, Sagemcom naturally stands out as a leading player in management solutions for energy and water that actively contribute to the more rational use of energy.
